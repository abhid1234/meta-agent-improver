What we learned

Four takeaways from a self-improving agent

01
Failures are free training data.
21 iterations, 2 proposer crashes, 1 regression epoch. Every stumble sharpened the next prompt.
02
Small models + good prompts rival larger ones.
Mistral 24B + the optimized prompt → 90% pass rate. The prompt is portable capital.
03
Prompt edits must be length-neutral.
Adding guidance without removing anything broke tasks that already passed. Attention budgets are real.
04
Hardware rewrites architecture; schedules survive.
Depth=8 loses on A40, wins on H100 by 0.046. But LR schedule changes help on every GPU.
